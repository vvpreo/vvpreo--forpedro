Are you looking for a delicious way to spice up your weekday dinner? Look no further than this quick and easy stir-fry recipe! With just a few fresh ingredients and a splash of soy sauce, you can whip up a healthy meal that’s sure to impress. Start by chopping up your favorite veggies — think bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a skillet, toss in the veggies, and stir-fry for about 5 minutes. For added protein, throw in some diced chicken or tofu. Once everything's cooked to your liking, drizzle with soy sauce and serve over a steaming bowl of rice. Enjoy your delightful dinner and the compliments that come with it!
