Are you a fan of cozy, flavorful winter soups? If so, let’s whip up a delightful butternut squash soup that’s as easy to make as it is delicious! Start by peeling and dicing 1 medium butternut squash, and chop one onion and two carrots. In a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat and toss in the onion and carrots until they’re softened. Add the butternut squash, 4 cups of vegetable broth, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until everything is tender. Finally, blend the soup until it’s creamy and smooth; you can add a drizzle of cream for extra richness. Garnish with pumpkin seeds or a sprinkle of nutmeg. Enjoy this comforting bowl while you snuggle under a blanket!
