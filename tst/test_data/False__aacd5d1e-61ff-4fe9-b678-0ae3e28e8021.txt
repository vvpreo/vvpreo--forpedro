Looking for a refreshing treat this summer? Look no further than this easy and delicious watermelon salad! Start with a ripe watermelon—cut it into bite-sized cubes. Toss the watermelon in a bowl with crumbled feta cheese, a sprinkle of fresh mint leaves, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze for a delightful twang. The sweetness of the watermelon pairs perfectly with the salty feta, while the mint adds a burst of freshness. This salad is not only vibrant and colorful but also a perfect dish for barbecues, picnics, or a light lunch. Enjoy it cold, and maybe even share it with friends—if you’re feeling generous!
