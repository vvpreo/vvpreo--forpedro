If you're looking for a delightful way to bring a little sunshine into your kitchen, look no further than this refreshing lemonade recipe! It's super simple and perfect for warm days when you want to cool off. Just grab four lemons, one cup of sugar, and four cups of water. Start by juicing the lemons into a measuring cup, ensuring to remove any seeds along the way. In a saucepan, combine the sugar and one cup of water over medium heat, stirring until dissolved to create a simple syrup. Next, mix that syrup with the lemon juice and the remaining water in a pitcher. Taste it; if you like it sweeter, feel free to add a little more sugar! Chill it in the fridge, and serve with ice and fresh mint leaves for that extra flair. Enjoy your refreshing homemade lemonade!
